# Madrid Insider Constitution

> **Amended 2026-07-20** — Article I revised to make Madrid Now the
> homepage content and reduce top-level navigation to 4 categories.
> See the Amendment Log at the bottom for the full history.

## Article I — Homepage Navigation

The homepage of Madrid Insider serves two purposes:

1. **Madrid Now** — live time, weather, current events, transit alerts,
   and top news. This is the *content* of the homepage.
2. **Four ways in** — the four permanent navigation categories that
   link to the rest of the site:

   1. Madrid Essentials
   2. Living in Madrid
   3. See in Madrid
   4. Do in Madrid

The four categories appear in the header bar on every page. Madrid Now
content appears as a sidebar/section on the homepage and as a full
category page at `/now`.

The fifth category — **Madrid Now** — still exists in the data model
and is accessible at `/now`, but it is not part of the top-level
navigation. Its content is featured on the homepage.

## Article II — Mission

Madrid Insider exists to serve English-speaking visitors, students,
expats, and residents of Madrid.

Tagline: **ONE-STOP SITE ALL ABOUT MADRID**

## Article III — Geographic Scope

Madrid takes precedence over Spain-wide content.

Preferred examples:
- From Madrid to Toledo
- From Madrid to Segovia
- From Madrid to El Escorial

## Article IV — Content Priorities

1. English-friendly services.
2. Official sources.
3. Evergreen content.
4. Historical Madrid Insider knowledge.

## Article V — Content Status

Every record should be classified as:
- Evergreen
- Annual Review
- Dynamic
- Legacy
- Archived

## Article VI — Repository Roles

- madridinsider: Production site.
- madridinsider-data: Canonical database.
- madridinsider-lab: Experiments.

## Article VII — AI Agents

All AI agents must read:
- README.md
- ROADMAP.md
- HOMEPAGE_VISION.md
- AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS.md
- CONTRIBUTING.md

Failure to comply with these documents constitutes a violation of project standards.

  "Madrid Now" brings the user back to `/` (the homepage). The homepage

  Live Info section. Rationale: Madrid Now IS the homepage — the 4
  categories are nested prominently in the upper center as a portal,
