# Madrid Insider Homepage Vision

## Primary Message

Madrid Insider is the English-language operating manual for Madrid.

Secondary tagline:

> Everything you need to Visit, Live, and Thrive in Madrid.

---

## Homepage User Journey

The homepage should guide users through the following experience:

1. ARRIVE
2. SURVIVE
3. LIVE
4. EXPLORE
5. ENJOY

### ARRIVE
- Airport
- Metro
- SIM Cards
- Left Luggage

### SURVIVE
- Safety
- Money
- Emergency Numbers
- Basic Spanish

### LIVE
- Housing
- Healthcare
- NIE/TIE
- Banking

### EXPLORE
- Museums
- Neighborhoods
- Day Trips
- Parks

### ENJOY
- Food
- Nightlife
- Football
- Festivals

---

## User Personas

The homepage should immediately answer:

- I'm Visiting
- I'm Moving Here
- I'm a Student
- I'm an Expat
- I'm Here This Weekend

---

## Content Rules

- Madrid first.
- Spain second.
- Prioritize English-speaking services.
- Prefer official links.
- Favor interconnected records.
